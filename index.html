About

Hi! My name is Reuel Jonathan, and I am a software developer based on Brazil.

I started my career at 17 as an apprentice developer. Since there I passed through many projects of different types: Web applications, embedded applications and desktop applications.

I consider myself a developer of generalist profile, so I try to know at least a little about many things, and it has helped me a lot through the challenges I faced (and still face) on this fascinating area of computing.

Talking about the fascinating area of computing, I like to (try to) understand how everything works under the hood, as there is no magic in computing. Just abstractions.
